What is Lingzhi?

Lingzhi (other names: Ganoderma Lucidum and Reishi), a member of the family of Fungi, is one of the most precious and rare wild plants in China and has also been called mushroom of immortality. Lingzhi has been used in China for more than 2000 years. In the past Lingzhi was difficult to grow and find. However, in 1970s the breakthrough technology allowed to cultivate Lingzhi commercially. Today Lingzhi products are used commonly as food supplements in Asian countries. It is considered as a magic herb for human body.
